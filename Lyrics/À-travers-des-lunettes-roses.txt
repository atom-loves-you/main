*life in pink:
***
Directly translated to "life in pink", "La vie en rose" essentially means seeing life through rose-coloured glasses.

Living with an attitude or outlook of positivity, trying to see beauty in the everyday; like you do when you first fall in love.

"À travers des lunettes roses", written and performed by Khristin Schenk, July 2024.
