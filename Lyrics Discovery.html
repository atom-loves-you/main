Behind the Music

Inspiration

Khristin Schenk shared that "Stay" was inspired by a period of personal transformation and setting boundaries in relationships. The lyrics reflect the internal conflict between attachment and the need for independence.

Recording Process

Recorded in a home studio in April 2025, the track features layered vocals that create a haunting echo effect. The minimalistic instrumentation was intentional to let the raw emotion in the vocals shine through.

Fun Fact

The song was originally titled "Distance" but was changed to "Stay" during the final mixing session. The haunting synth sound in the background was created using a vintage 1980s synthesizer that Khristin found at a flea market.
