Strawberry Everything

written by Khristin Schenk (Atom Loves You) October 31, 2024


Spun sugar and bleach,
Strawberry everything.


You’ll stay green, after
All day in the sun,
Golden and untouchable, quiet light dancing through trees,
Push and pull, you’re a riot of thoughts, scorn backed with envy,

A few photons displaced. They tried to wave you on, but still, I
can’t feel sad
For all the time I’ve been gone.


Spun sugar and bleach,
Strawberry everything.


The sunset strokes a new skyline tonight with amber fire,
The world turned in, just for me—
I thought I said it all out loud,
But maybe I just dream too deep.
You bring the warmth, I bring the coal,
Spun sugar and bleed.


Mama’s lost at sea, found by perfect harmony,
Bound together on gossamer wings.
Stars above us never sleep,
In whispered tones, my heart sings


Spun sugar and bleach,
Strawberry everything
